Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MjengoPay.

Workers dial *123# on any mobile phone, then follow the prompts to register their name, skills, and location. Once registered, they'll receive SMS notifications about relevant jobs in their area.
While the dashboard experience is best on a smartphone or computer, foremen can also access core features via SMS and USSD. We designed MjengoPay to be accessible to everyone regardless of device.
Payments are processed securely through mobile money providers like M-Pesa. Foremen initiate payments through the dashboard, workers receive the money instantly, and both parties get SMS confirmations.
MjengoPay is free for small construction teams (up to 5 workers). For larger teams, we offer affordable monthly plans with additional features like advanced reporting and worker rating systems.
